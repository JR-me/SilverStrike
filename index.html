Welcome to SilverStrike!
An on-chain idle clicker game. Every click mines real SILVER tokens on LiteForge — one transaction per mine.

Upgrade Your Tools
Start with a Pickaxe (1 SILVER/click). Mine 100 SILVER to unlock the Drill (3/click), then 400 for the Excavator (9/click).
🏗️
Max Out & Compete
Hit the Excavator and grind to the top of the leaderboard. The most dedicated miners can claim rare NFT badges — including the coveted God tier.
🏆
Earn Badges
Reach SILVER milestones to claim on-chain badge NFTs: Common, Rare, Mythical, Legendary, and the ultimate God badge for true grinders.
